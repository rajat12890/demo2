# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience.

## Our Standards
We expect all participants to follow these standards of behavior.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing the code of conduct.

## Scope
This code of conduct applies within all community spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported.

## Enforcement Guidelines
All reports will be reviewed and investigated fairly and promptly.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.0.
